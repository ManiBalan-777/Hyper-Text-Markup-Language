ABOUT THE COLLEGE:

Government College of Engineering, Sengipatti, Thanjavur was started in the year 2013 by Government of Tamilnadu to cater for the needs of technical education in emerging areas of engineering and excellence in research in different disciplines of science and engineering. The Tamil Nadu Government has created adequate infrastructural facilities and sufficient funds and is keen on developing the institute for higher education. The college is located on 35.73 acres of lush green environment,20 km from the city of Thanjavur and 35 km from the city of Trichy on Sengipatti - Gandharvakottai Main road. This college is approved by AICTE,New Delhi and affiliated to Anna University, Chennai. This college is the recognized research centre of Anna University, Chennai.

Our institution consists of 30 smart classrooms, I-Studio for online lecture creation, Video broadcasting system, Digital class room,6 Seminar Halls, 60 laboratories, and a separate workshop. Added to this, it has a spacious Auditorium, Boys Hostel, Girls Hostel, Spacious Play ground, multi-gym, and Library. The institution offers the following courses
